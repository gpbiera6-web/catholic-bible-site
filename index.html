The Catholic Bible is a sacred collection of inspired writings used by the Roman Catholic Church. It teaches faith, morals, history, and God's relationship with humanity.

What Is the Catholic Bible?

  • Contains 73 books
  • 46 Old Testament books
  • 27 New Testament books
  • Includes the Deuterocanonical books

The Deuterocanonical books are Tobit, Judith, Wisdom, Sirach, Baruch, 1 Maccabees, and 2 Maccabees.

Books of the Catholic Bible

Old Testament (46 Books)

Law: Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy

Historical: Joshua, Judges, Ruth, 1–2 Samuel, 1–2 Kings, 1–2 Chronicles, Ezra, Nehemiah, Tobit, Judith, Esther, 1–2 Maccabees

Wisdom: Job, Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, Song of Songs, Wisdom, Sirach

Prophets: Isaiah, Jeremiah, Lamentations, Baruch, Ezekiel, Daniel, Hosea, Joel, Amos, Obadiah, Jonah, Micah, Nahum, Habakkuk, Zephaniah, Haggai, Zechariah, Malachi

New Testament (27 Books)

Matthew, Mark, Luke, John, Acts, Romans, 1–2 Corinthians, Galatians, Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians, 1–2 Thessalonians, 1–2 Timothy, Titus, Philemon, Hebrews, James, 1–2 Peter, 1–3 John, Jude, Revelation

  • B. 70
  • C. 73

2. Which of the following is a Deuterocanonical book?

  • A. Genesis
  • B. Wisdom

Answer: Wisdom

3. The Catholic Bible is based mainly on the:

  • A. Latin Dictionary
  • B. Hebrew Bible only

You Are Welcome Here

This website exists as a quiet place not to argue, force, or judge but to offer understanding, rest, and truth with gentleness.

If you feel distant from faith, unsure of what you believe, or simply tired, you are not broken. Questions are allowed. Doubt is not failure. You are welcome exactly as you are.

A Prayer for Those Who Feel Lost

This prayer is offered gently, for anyone seeking peace, clarity, and a path back to hope.

Lord God,

For those who feel confused, distant, or unsure of where they belong, grant light to their minds and peace to their hearts.

When faith feels heavy and the way forward is unclear, remind them that they are loved, seen, and never alone.

Lead them with patience, not fear; with truth, not pressure; and with mercy that heals rather than judges.

May they find rest in Your presence, courage in their questions, and hope in Your unfailing love.

Amen.

A Prayer for Doubt and Healing

God of mercy,

When belief feels fragile and answers feel far away, meet us in the middle of our uncertainty.

Teach us that doubt can be a doorway, not a wall — a place where faith grows deeper and more honest.

Heal wounds caused by misunderstanding, fear, or judgment. Restore trust where it has been broken.

Walk with us patiently, until hope feels possible again.

Amen.
